About our video downloader

This tool lets you save videos from YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and hundreds of other platforms directly to your device — no software to install, no account to create. Just paste a link and download.

How to download YouTube videos

Go to YouTube, find the video you want to save, and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. Come back here, paste it into the box at the top of the page, and click Download. The video will be saved in the best quality available.

Saving Instagram reels and posts

On Instagram, tap the three dots (⋯) on a post or reel, select "Copy link", then paste that link into the downloader above. We support public posts, reels, and IGTV videos.

Downloading TikTok videos

Open the TikTok video you want to save, tap Share, and copy the link. Paste it here and hit Download. We fetch the original file directly from TikTok's servers.

Supported formats and devices

Downloaded files are typically in MP4 format, which plays on virtually every device and media player. The tool works on:

  • iPhone and iPad (Safari, Chrome)
  • Android phones and tablets
  • Windows PC (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Mac (Safari, Chrome, Firefox)
  • Linux (any modern browser)

Troubleshooting tips

If a download doesn't work, try these steps:

  • Make sure the video is publicly accessible
  • Copy the full URL, not just the domain
  • Try a different browser
  • Check the video isn't age-restricted or private
